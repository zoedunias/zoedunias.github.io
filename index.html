Master thesis

Currently, I am working on my master thesis at the Julius Center, UMC Utrecht, under supervision of Click Maarten van Smeden and Ben Van Calster. The aim of this project is to systematically compare hyperparameter tuning procedures for clinical prediction models and machine learning algorithms in low-dimensional data. Simulation studies, varying various data-characteristics, will be performed to evaluate out-of-sample predictive performance of clinical prediction models for dichotomous risk prediction. This study aims to identify optimal tuning procedures relative to the specific data characteristics at hand, in order to provide guidance to applied modelers. The project runs from September 2020 until June 2021.

MASAS

I am excited to be involved in the Methodology And Statistics Alumni Society (MASAS) foundation for students and alumni of the Methodology and Statistics master’s programme at Utrecht University. MASAS aspires to build a community around the master’s programme. By means of a LinkedIn group and by organizing two to three activities a year, MASAS has the goal to stimulate and facilitate professional and interpersonal contact between (former) students. I have joined the board of the MASAS foundation since 2019, filling the position of vice president and secretary. This means I organise events together with my fellow board members and am the main contact point.
